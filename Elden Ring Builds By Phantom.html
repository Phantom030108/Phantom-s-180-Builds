    • Daggers:

    • Dagger (Int) (Carian Grandeur)

      • Used to cast Carian Grandeur only. If you wish to attack melee too consider Magic infused Bloodstained, if you wish to powerstance, drop the 2nd dagger with sacred order for a second Magic infused Bloodstained with Carian Granduer in left hand for spamming, and endure in right hand for powerstance attacks.

    • Black Knife (Fai)

      • Used to spam its ash of war only. If you want to attack melee, use the Erdsteel Dagger build and set it to sacred affinity.

    • Misericorde (Int)

      • Cast Phalanx with left Weapon (can be a dagger instead of a misericorde) until stance broken. Use royal knight's resolve on right hand misericorde and attack. If you wish to powerstance, use the Great Knife build.

    • Reduvia (Arc)

      • Used to spam its ash of war only. If you want to attack melee, use the Bloodstained build

    • Glintstone Kris (Int)

      • Used to spam its ash of war only. If you want to attack melee, follow text guide under Dagger build
